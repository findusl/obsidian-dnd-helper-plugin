Detailed Demographics

Hanrane is built upon an isolated peak that can only be accessed by a bridge, and is known for its mines and industry. Town is ruled by a priest of the local religion (Dondon Silvereyes, Male Halfling). The races live together mostly in harmony.

Defenses: The town is surrounded by rammed-earth walls. 14 trained warriors serve in the town's defense, and a militia of 256 can be mustered in case of attack. The sheriff is Thea Kettlewhistle, Female Halfling.
